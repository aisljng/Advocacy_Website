Save the Arts!

Art education is a critical aspect students need in order to thrive in their envirnoment. However there is a serious lack of funding for arts in schools.

More than 80% of schools funding has been cut since 2008. As a result schools are forced to decide what aspects of education are deemed as more valuable. This has caused schools to cut arts from the curriculum meaning classes such as music, theater, dance, and all other art related classes are being put to the side or are being overall eleminted from schools. This is a problem because art is an essiential skill people need to create innovative ideas.

Research Shows:

The beneficial affects art curriculum has on students

  • Low-income students who are highly engaged in the arts are more than twice as likely to graduate college as their peers with no arts education.
  • Students involved in arts are 4 times more likely to participate in a math and science fair
  • Lower droupout rates
